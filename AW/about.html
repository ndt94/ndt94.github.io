Về chúng tôi

What started as a dorm room project in October of 2014 has become HVW – not only the best darn vintage watch retail store, but a leading content producer and thought leader in the world of watch commentary. Our content exists to shake the wristwatch industry, to create a new, entirely raw and transparent forum for discussion, with daily releases as entertaining as they are informative. We’re dedicated to taking up your commute, lunch break, and before dinner “reading”, while offering a collection of endlessly interesting vintage Rolexe
